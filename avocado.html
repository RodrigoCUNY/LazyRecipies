Simple Avocado Toast Recipe

Here are some easy and quick recipe ideas similar to a simple Avocado Toast, perfect for lazy homebodies who want something delicious without too much effort.

This recipe is a very special breakfast meal; just look at the ingredients to see why. It has to be tasted to be believed.

The following ingredients make one serving:

  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 2 slices of bread (whole grain, sourdough, etc.)
  • A pinch of salt
  • A drizzle of olive oil or lemon juice
  • Optional: red pepper flakes, a fried egg, or tomato slices

Instructions

  1. Toast the bread.
  2. Mash the avocado in a bowl, season with salt, and mix in olive oil or lemon juice.
  3. Spread the avocado mixture onto the toast.
  4. Top with any optional ingredients like a fried egg or red pepper flakes.

You should only make a maximum of two servings per frying pan.
