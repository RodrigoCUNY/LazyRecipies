Simple Omelette Recipe

Here are some easy and quick recipe ideas similar to Omelette, perfect for lazy homebodies who want something delicious without too much effort.

Ingredients

  • 2-3 eggs
  • A handful of shredded cheese
  • Optional: diced tomatoes, spinach, onions, or ham
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Whisk the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper.
  2. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat, lightly oil it, and pour the egg mixture in.
  3. Let the eggs set slightly, then add cheese and any optional veggies or meats.
  4. Fold the omelet and cook for another minute.

You should only make a maximum of two servings per frying pan.
